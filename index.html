95, You STINK!

Oh, 95, always full, always congested.

You stress me out.

Although in the early morning you're not so bad,

In the afternoon, you stink!

5 o'clock, 6 o'clock, makes no difference. You really stink!

7 o'clock, you're much better, but that's too late.

Express Lanes, You're Cool, But You Cost Too Much

Oh, 95... Wait. What are those two lanes on your far left?

Cars are moving. Moving fast! Way faster than me!

I must drive on thee!

Oh, express lanes, in the morning you deprive me of $4.50. You stink!

5 o'clock, $10.00?!!! 6 o'clock, $7.50?!!! You really stink!

Express lanes, I can't afford you.

Bus, You Make Me As Happy As A Clam

Oh, express lanes... Wait. That bus moving fast on your asphalt, where is it going?

I must find out. I must ride on it!

95 Express bus to Miramar, you're my stress reliever.

You take me to LaunchCode and bring me home, all in an hour!

From your stop, I ride my bike only for five blocks!

Oh, 95 Express bus, you make me as happy as a clam.
